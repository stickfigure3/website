PARTNERSHIPS

Wall Street Prep, Inc.

Wall Street Prep is a leading training organization, established in 2004 by investment bankers to address the gap between academic knowledge and the real-world skills needed in the financial services industry. They offer both instructor-led and online training programs designed to teach the practical tools required to succeed on the job. With a global faculty of over 90 seasoned professionals—including investment bankers, private equity experts, and investment professionals—Wall Street Prep has become a trusted resource for anyone looking to advance their career in finance.

Wharton Online, University of Pennsylvania

Our partnership with Wharton Online combines the academic excellence of The Wharton School with a focus on delivering real-world, practical skills that employers value. This collaboration is designed to provide a unique blend of theory and hands-on application, equipping participants with the tools and knowledge necessary to excel in the competitive finance industry. Through Wharton Online, our team has benefited from a flexible learning environment supported by interactive features such as virtual office hours, one-on-one tutoring, and dynamic networking opportunities. These experiences have enhanced our understanding of financial principles while providing invaluable insights typically gained through years of industry mentorship. We are excited to channel this knowledge into the IBA club, creating a platform where members can bridge the gap between academic learning and practical application. By hosting workshops, discussions, and training sessions, we aim to empower our members with industry-relevant skills and prepare them to thrive in the ever-evolving world of finance.
