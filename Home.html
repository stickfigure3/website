ABOUT US

The Investment Banking Academy at UC Santa Cruz, a student-led organization dedicated to bringing the dynamic world of investment banking and high finance to our campus. At IBA, we are a learning community committed to providing students with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to explore careers in investment banking, and other fields like private equity, venture capital, and corporate finance. Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world financial practices, empowering students to succeed in competitive industries through hands-on experiences, case studies, and financial modeling courses.
